Four Architecture Options
All use a Directive-style nav: focused primary items + hamburger for secondary content + persistent CTA. Each has interactive nav demos — hover items and click the hamburger to explore.

Executive Summary

Solid Digital is repositioning from a web design and marketing agency to an AI transformation partner for mid-market companies. The team has endorsed the Brand → Performance → Integration → Innovation framework as the customer journey, agreed that services should lead with pain points rather than capabilities, and identified three client tiers (transactional, mid-level, full-service). All four architecture options below reflect these decisions while keeping the site credible for a team of six — focused primary nav, hamburger for secondary content, and no half-empty pages.

Why "Methodology" as the third item

Your biggest differentiator isn't what you do — it's how you do it. "Methodology" signals a structured, proven process — something a mid-market CEO evaluating you vs. a cheaper AI freelancer wants to see. It's where the 4 pillars, the Brand → Innovation framework, and 19 years of experience come together into a compelling trust-building page.

Dual CTAs without redundancy

"See Our Work" as a CTA button (not a nav item) links to the case studies page. "Start a Project" is the primary conversion action. No "Work" in the nav means no duplication — but proof is always one click away.

Why Industries in the primary nav

An Industries dropdown instantly signals vertical expertise to prospects. When a manufacturing CEO sees "Manufacturing & Industrial" in your nav, they know you understand their world — ERP integration, supply chain complexity, compliance. It filters out tire-kickers and attracts the right conversations. The 8 industries listed are based on your existing client base (healthcare, SaaS, nonprofits) plus high-opportunity verticals (manufacturing, financial services, real estate) where mid-market companies have the operational complexity and AI readiness gaps that Solid Digital is built to solve.

Why "Our Approach" alongside Industries

Industries tells prospects you understand their world. Our Approach tells them how you'll work together. Together with Services and Products, the four nav items create a complete picture: what you do, who you do it for, what they can use, and why you're different. The dual CTAs handle proof (See Our Work) and conversion (Start a Project) without needing either in the nav.
